Bean Walnut Mushroom Burgers

These Bean Walnut Mushroom Burgers hit every mark. They're hefty, have great texture with the walnuts and oats, and they can easily be made in advance and frozen.

Ingredient

Spices And Condiments

Veggies From The Pantry

Instructions

1

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Spray a cookie sheet with cooking spray. Toss the mushrooms with some salt and pepper.

2

Roast for 20 minutes or until the mushrooms are dry. Place the mushrooms in a food processor and pulse 5-10 times

3

Next, add the walnuts to the food processor bowl, pulse three to four more times until coarsely ground, then add the beans and the oats and pulse three to four times until everything is well mixed.

4

Add the ketchup, mustard, garlic powder, salt and black pepper to taste, and the thyme. Pulse a few more times until everything comes together.

5

Using your fingers, shape the mixture into eight patties.

6

Heat a skillet and coat with cooking spray. Place the patties on the skillet

7

Once the patties have cooled, you can separate with wax paper and freeze. Throw on the grill when ready to eat, or reheat and serve.
