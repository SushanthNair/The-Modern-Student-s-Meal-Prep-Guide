Keto Methi Malai Paneer Tofu

A fast and fabulously flavorful recipe for Instant Keto Methi Malai Paneer Tofu you can make in under 15 minutes.

Ingredient

Spices And Pulses

Veggies From The Pantry

Instructions

1

Blend the cashews with ¼ cup water and set aside.

2

Heat the oil and add the cumin seeds. As they begin to darken slightly and become fragrant, add the bell peppers.

3

Saute the peppers three to four minutes or until they soften a bit

4

Then add the kasoori methi, stir it in, then add tomato sauce and salt to taste.

5

Add the tofu and mix.

6

When the sauce comes to a boil, add the cashew paste and stir it in.

7

Turn off the heat and serve. Garnish with cilantro, if you wish.
